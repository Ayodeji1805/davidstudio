About the Author

Segun David Olowogbon is a passionate young author from Nigeria. Inspired by his parents — Dr. Toyin Samuel Olowogbon and Mrs. Omolola Faith Olowogbon — he writes meaningful stories that empower children, strengthen families, and inspire wise decisions.
